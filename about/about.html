I’m Mathew K. Paul, also known as Chicknvictim, and I create under the name Chickn Victim when it comes to digital work. Born on June 16, 2004, my main focus is 3D modeling, texturing, rendering, and unwrapping—pretty much everything that brings a model to life. I’ve also been into pencil drawing for a long time, which helps with how I see forms and details. On the side, I experiment with UI/UX design, build static webpages, and practice calligraphy (still unlocking new fonts). I work with Maya, Substance Painter, Marmoset, Keyshot, Unreal Engine 5, and Photoshop—always learning, always improving, and always feeling like there’s a patch update waiting for me.
