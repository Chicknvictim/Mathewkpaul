Audi r8

I created this Audi as a tribute to mark the end of its production. The R8's clean, modern design and balanced performance continue to resonate with enthusiasts. With its mid-engine layout and sleek lines, it offers a dynamic yet accessible driving experience that has left a lasting impact on automotive design. Here are some renders made with the KeyShot render engine.

"The Audi R8 is a high-performance sports car that strikes a balance between thrilling performance and everyday usability. Renowned for its mid-engine layout and sleek, aerodynamic design, the R8 is powered by a formidable V10 engine that delivers an exhilarating driving experience. Its advanced engineering and precision handling are complemented by a luxurious interior, making it both a track-ready machine and a comfortable ride for daily driving. With its cutting-edge technology and striking design, the R8 has cemented its reputation as a modern automotive icon that continues to inspire car enthusiasts worldwide.

This is my first car model, completed in a couple of months. I handled the modeling and texturing in Maya and rendered it in Arnold, aiming for a realistic look. I used reference images to nail the details and experimented with different materials and lighting setups to capture the car’s true character. As I progressed, I learned how to refine everything from the smooth curves of the body to the reflective surfaces that give the model depth. This project not only challenged me technically but also helped expand my knowledge of Maya and 3D design. Here are some renders taken from Arnold that showcase the journey and effort behind this model.
